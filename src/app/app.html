Agata's Camino Journey

Support the Journey 💜

More than 1 in 3 women in Ireland 35% have experienced psychological, physical, or sexual abuse from an intimate partner.

On the 15th of May, I’ll be flying to Porto to walk roughly 270km of the Portuguese Coastal Camino de Santiago to raise funds for Esker House Domestic Abuse Support Service.

This cause is deeply personal to me. I’m walking to raise awareness, to honour my mams strength, and to help ensure more people find safety. I hope that by raising awarness, more people will find the strength and courage to ask for help.

Doing this for every person who has experienced abuse, and for those who continue to suffer in silence.
Most of all, I'm doing it for my mam 💜

Esker House provides vital 24‑hour support to people experiencing domestic violence, helping them find safety, strength, and a way forward. Every donation helps them continue their life‑changing work.
If this journey resonates with you, please consider donating your spare change to support the vital work of Esker House.
